Arena Data CSV Import Window

STEP 1: Install the REFlex - Arena/Battleground Historian addon from https://wow.curseforge.com

STEP 2: Dominate in the arena (or battlegrounds, if you're into that sort of thing)

STEP 3: Click on the REFlex icon by your minimap and click on the [D] button to open the csv data

STEP 4: Select all (control + a) and then copy to your clipboard (control + c)

STEP 5: Come back to this window and paste (control + p) into the text box above

STEP 6: Click on IMPORT
